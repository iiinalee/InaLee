2020, is Taiwan’s presidential election. At that time, one of the candidates for reelection, Ms. Tsai Ing-wen proposed a political policy of "0-6 years old, Raised by the country".

Her Political opinions proposed that the scale of childcare services will continue to be expanded. And the budget will increase from about 60 billion dollar to 100 billion dollar annually to increase the supply of affordable childcare services, increase childcare subsidies and childcare allowances, and so on.

And we hope that through this political opinion animation, the public can understand political opinions. In this project, I am an art designer.

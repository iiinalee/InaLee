In the 2018 ZaShare School Exhibition, UDSTAND hold an little exibition with the topic of "sex". We created two interactive games focusing on the female period. One of game is "Egg’s Adventure", and I am the art designer of this game.

The game uses a joystick with the white and blue device as the operation. Players play the role of the female ovum and will race in the game. From the small follicle at the beginning, players will gradually grow into the female ovum. In the end, you will become old and decline and then reach the end.

There are some different foods during the race, and players need to eat the right foods to get higer scores.

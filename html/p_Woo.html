"WOO" is a Role Playing Game using the courtship of animals as the main concept. Players will acting animal's behavior like singing, fighting or dancing to understand their way of courtship in the game.

We use colorful visual and animal's exaggerated actions to increase fun during the play. Let everybody easily learn about animal's knowledge through this interesting and unique theme to achieve the effort of entertaining and educational.

This project is my graduation production. I am a designer of 3D modeling, meanwhile I learn more about Cinema 4D skills. Our team does not only participate in the campus exhibition, and has also signed up for various competitions to make more people know about the knowledge of animals.

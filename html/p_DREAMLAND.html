This is a project that I designed when I was an exchange student in Germany. It’s mainly to share me and friend’s figure and toy collections with everyone.

Our collections can be roughly divided into four categories: Mike Wazowski, Kyorochan, Minions and others.

In each page, we provide the basic information about the figures on the website for everyone to understand.

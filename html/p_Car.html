This project is a redesign of the TSIB Car Loan official website. In addition to the home page design, the application process pages was also included.

In key visual's slideshow on home page, I have creatively designed something different and special. The key visual's concept is to see the opposite car through the front window from the driver's perspective, and different cars represent main products of the loan. What's more, through the swing of the wiper, it could change slideshow's pictures and make the slideshow more interesting.

The visual style of the website uses cusp parallelograms to present the of speed of the car, with the soft illustration style to neutralize the smoothness of the website. In addition to the web design, I also drew lots of illustrations and designed the input guideline of the process pages.
